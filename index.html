person About Me

I am a seasoned cybersecurity professional with expertise in Information Security, Digital Forensics, and Incident Response, complemented by a background in Linguistics and Text Analytics. I have led impactful workshops for organizations like the Nigeria Police and UBEC, specializing in ethical hacking, penetration testing, and security assessments to help businesses strengthen their defenses. Alongside cybersecurity, I have significant experience in web development, delivering security-focused website projects, WordPress overhauls, and ongoing support. Holding a Master’s degree in Information Security and Digital Forensics from the University of East London and currently expanding my knowledge through a Cybersecurity training program at BeCode in Brussels, I am passionate about staying ahead of cybersecurity trends. With strong leadership and communication skills, I manage client projects and cross-functional teams effectively, and in January 2025, I will be launching Gue Cyber Labs, my new cybersecurity venture.
