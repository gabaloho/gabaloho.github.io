I hold a Master’s degree in Information Security and Digital Forensics from the University of East London and am currently enhancing my skills through a Cybersecurity training program at BeCode in Brussels. With a passion for staying ahead of emerging cybersecurity trends, I am constantly expanding my knowledge to provide innovative solutions for businesses in an increasingly digital world.

With a track record of leading cross-functional teams, managing client projects, and delivering results under pressure, I am committed to making meaningful contributions to any organization I collaborate with. I am also gearing up to launch my own cybersecurity venture, Gue Cyber Labs, in January 2025, further expanding my entrepreneurial journey.
